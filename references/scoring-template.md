# Long-term scoring template (100 points)

## Weights
- Growth quality: 30
- Financial resilience: 25
- Valuation sanity: 20
- Filing/governance risk: 25

## Scoring rubric

### 1) Growth quality (0-30)
- 24-30: Multi-year growth with consistent profitability quality.
- 15-23: Mixed trend, acceptable but uneven.
- 0-14: Weak/unstable trend or low visibility.

### 2) Financial resilience (0-25)
- 20-25: Healthy balance of leverage and cash generation.
- 12-19: Manageable risk, but needs monitoring.
- 0-11: Fragile structure or deteriorating cash profile.

### 3) Valuation sanity (0-20)
- 16-20: Reasonable vs growth/quality.
- 8-15: Fair but not clearly attractive.
- 0-7: Rich valuation unsupported by fundamentals.

### 4) Filing/governance risk (0-25)
- 20-25: Clean filing pattern, no major unresolved concerns.
- 10-19: Some concerns, monitor closely.
- 0-9: Serious unresolved filing/governance/legal risks.

## Thresholds
- 75-100: Pass (candidate)
- 60-74: Watch (monitor, no immediate conviction)
- 0-59: Drop (avoid for now)

## Override rules
Force **Drop** regardless of score when severe unresolved risk exists:
- Auditor/going-concern red flags
- Material internal-control weakness without remediation credibility
- Ongoing shareholder-hostile dilution pattern
- Major unresolved legal/regulatory threat

## Output format
- Total score: `__/100`
- Decision: `Pass | Watch | Drop`
- Confidence: `High | Medium | Low`
- Top 3 reasons
- Red flags
- Next review trigger
